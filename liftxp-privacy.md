# Privacy Policy for LiftXP

**Effective date: July 18, 2026**

LiftXP: Ranked Gym Workouts (“LiftXP,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is a workout, strength, body-weight, and nutrition tracking app.

This Privacy Policy explains what information LiftXP handles, how it is used, and what choices you have.

## 1. Data Stored on Your Device

LiftXP does not currently require you to create an account.

Your regular app data is stored locally on your device. This may include:

* Workout routines
* Exercises, sets, repetitions, and weights
* Workout history
* Personal records and estimated one-repetition maximums
* Exercise and muscle-group ranks
* Body-weight entries
* Nutrition and macronutrient entries
* Progress graphs
* App preferences

This information is not uploaded to a LiftXP database.

Because the information is stored locally, deleting LiftXP from your device removes its locally stored data. Reinstalling the app starts with a new local data set unless your device or operating system restores app data from a backup.

LiftXP does not currently provide an account-based cloud backup or synchronization service.

## 2. AI Features

LiftXP includes optional AI-powered features for:

* Generating workouts based on your goals
* Logging meals from text
* Logging meals using voice input
* Logging meals from a photo

When you use one of these features, the information needed to process your request is sent to OpenRouter and processed using Google Gemini models.

Depending on the feature, this information may include:

* Your workout goals and preferences
* A written description of a meal
* A meal photo
* A voice recording or transcription
* Information included in your AI request

LiftXP uses zero-data-retention settings for these AI requests. OpenRouter is configured to route requests through providers that support zero data retention.

AI features are optional. You can continue using LiftXP’s manual workout and nutrition logging features without submitting information to an AI model.

Do not include sensitive personal, medical, financial, or confidential information in an AI request.

## 3. Meal Photos

When you take a meal photo inside LiftXP for AI meal logging, the image is held in memory as needed to process the request.

When you select an existing image from your photo library, LiftXP reads the image you selected for meal recognition. LiftXP does not delete or modify the original image in your photo library.

Your photo library remains under your control. You can delete original images through your device’s Photos app.

LiftXP only requests camera or photo-library access when you choose to use a feature that requires it.

## 4. Voice Input

When you use voice meal logging, LiftXP records the audio needed to process your request.

Voice recordings may be stored temporarily in the app’s cache. Cached files may remain until they are cleared by the app, the operating system, available device-storage management, or removal of the app.

LiftXP does not use voice recordings for advertising or user profiling.

You can avoid voice processing by typing your meal manually.

LiftXP only requests microphone access when you choose to use voice logging.

## 5. Analytics

LiftXP uses PostHog to understand whether users complete the app’s onboarding process.

LiftXP sends manually created onboarding events to PostHog. PostHog session replay and automatic interaction capture are disabled.

The analytics information may include:

* Whether an onboarding step was completed
* An anonymous app or installation identifier
* IP address
* Approximate location derived from the IP address
* Device type
* Operating system and version
* App version
* Language, locale, screen, and similar technical information

LiftXP does not intentionally send your workouts, body-weight entries, nutrition entries, meal descriptions, meal photos, voice recordings, or other user-entered content to PostHog.

PostHog analytics are used to measure and improve the onboarding experience. They are not used for third-party advertising.

## 6. Exercise Rankings and Percentiles

LiftXP calculates exercise ranks, muscle-group rankings, and performance percentiles using general strength standards.

Your performance is not compared against personal data collected from other LiftXP users.

These results may use information such as:

* Exercise
* Weight lifted
* Repetitions completed
* Estimated one-repetition maximum
* Body weight, where relevant
* General strength standards

Ranks, percentiles, and estimated one-repetition maximums are estimates. Results may differ depending on technique, equipment, exercise variation, range of motion, fatigue, and the accuracy of the information entered.

## 7. Apple Health

LiftXP does not currently connect to Apple Health and does not read from or write information to HealthKit.

## 8. Subscriptions and Payments

LiftXP may offer auto-renewable subscriptions. Some subscription options may include a free trial.

Purchases, billing, free-trial eligibility, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are handled by Apple through the App Store.

LiftXP does not receive or store your full payment-card information.

You can manage or cancel a subscription through your Apple account:

**Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → LiftXP**

Deleting LiftXP does not automatically cancel an active App Store subscription.

LiftXP handles information to:

* Save your workouts and routines locally
* Track strength and body-weight progress
* Calculate estimated one-repetition maximums
* Generate exercise and muscle-group rankings
* Display progress graphs
* Track calories and macronutrients
* Generate workouts when requested
* Recognize and estimate meals when requested
* Measure onboarding completion
* Operate, protect, troubleshoot, and improve the app
* Respond to support and privacy requests
* Comply with applicable legal obligations

## 11. Data Sharing

LiftXP does not sell your personal information.

Information is shared only when needed to:

* Process an AI feature you requested
* Measure onboarding completion
* Process an App Store purchase
* Comply with a valid legal obligation
* Protect LiftXP, its users, or others from fraud, abuse, or security threats

LiftXP does not use third-party advertising networks.

## 12. Data Retention and Deletion

Workout, weight, nutrition, routine, and progress information is stored locally on your device.

To delete LiftXP’s locally stored data, delete the LiftXP app from your device. Reinstalling the app begins with a new local data set unless information is restored through a device backup.

Voice recordings may remain temporarily in the app’s cache until the cache is cleared or the app is removed.

Meal images taken for processing are held in memory as needed for the request. Original images selected from your photo library remain in your photo library until you delete them yourself.

AI requests are processed using zero-data-retention settings.

Limited analytics events may be retained by PostHog according to LiftXP’s analytics configuration and PostHog’s applicable retention practices.

You can change permissions at any time through your device settings. Disabling a permission may prevent the related feature from working, but manual logging options may remain available.

## 16. Fitness, Nutrition, and AI Disclaimer

LiftXP is a fitness and nutrition tracking tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment and is not a medical device.

Workout suggestions, strength rankings, nutrition targets, food estimates, and AI-generated results may be inaccurate or unsuitable for your individual circumstances.

Review AI-generated workouts before performing them. Check meal estimates before saving them.

Consult a qualified healthcare, fitness, or nutrition professional before making significant changes to your exercise or diet, especially if you have an injury, medical condition, dietary restriction, or other health concern.

Stop exercising and seek appropriate medical attention if you experience pain, dizziness, difficulty breathing, or other concerning symptoms.

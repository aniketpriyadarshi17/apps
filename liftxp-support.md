## Workout Data and Progress

### Where is my LiftXP data stored?

Workout routines, logged sets, body-weight entries, nutrition entries, and progress information are stored locally on your device.

LiftXP does not currently require an account or provide cloud synchronization.

### How do I delete my LiftXP data?

Delete LiftXP from your device to remove the data stored locally by the app.

Your operating system or device backup may retain a backup copy depending on your personal backup settings.

Deleting the app does not cancel an active subscription.

### Can I recover my data after deleting the app?

LiftXP does not currently provide its own cloud backup or account recovery system.

Data may not be recoverable after the app is deleted unless your device restores it from an existing backup.

## Rankings and Strength Percentiles

### How are my rankings calculated?

LiftXP compares your estimated strength against general strength standards. It does not compare your personal information against a database of other LiftXP users.

Rankings may use your exercise, weight, repetitions, estimated one-repetition maximum, and body weight where relevant.

### Why does a rank or percentile look incorrect?

Check that you entered:

* The correct exercise variation
* The correct weight unit
* The correct weight
* The number of completed repetitions
* Your current body weight, where required

Strength rankings and estimated one-repetition maximums are estimates. Equipment, technique, range of motion, fatigue, and exercise variations can affect the result.

## AI Workout Generation

### How does AI workout generation work?

LiftXP sends the goals and workout information needed for your request through OpenRouter to a Google Gemini model.

Zero-data-retention settings are enabled for these requests.

Review every generated workout before performing it. Adjust exercises, sets, repetitions, and weights based on your ability, equipment, injuries, and recovery.

### The generated workout does not fit my needs

Try updating the goal or information included in your request.

AI-generated workouts can make mistakes and are not medical advice. Do not perform an exercise that appears unsafe or unsuitable for you.

## AI Meal Logging

### How can I log a meal?

LiftXP may allow you to log a meal by:

* Typing a description
* Using voice input
* Taking a meal photo
* Selecting a photo from your library

The information is sent through OpenRouter to a Google Gemini model to estimate the meal’s calories and macronutrients.

### The meal estimate is wrong

AI meal recognition cannot always identify exact serving sizes, ingredients, sauces, cooking oils, or preparation methods.

Review the result before saving it and manually correct the values when needed.

### Why does LiftXP need camera access?

Camera access is used only when you choose to photograph a meal for AI logging.

### Why does LiftXP need photo-library access?

Photo-library access lets you select an existing meal photo.

LiftXP does not delete or modify the original image in your photo library.

### Why does LiftXP need microphone access?

Microphone access is used when you choose voice meal logging.

You can use text input instead.

## Subscription Support

### How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

LiftXP subscriptions are handled by Apple.

On your iPhone, open:

**Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → LiftXP**

Select the subscription to view its status or cancellation options.

### Does deleting LiftXP cancel my subscription?

No. Removing the app does not cancel an active subscription.

Manage or cancel the subscription separately through your Apple account.

### How do free trials work?

Some LiftXP subscription options may include a free trial.

The trial duration, price after the trial, and renewal terms are displayed by Apple before you confirm the purchase.

Unless cancelled before the trial ends, the subscription may automatically renew at the displayed price.

### How do I request a refund?

App Store transactions and refund requests are handled by Apple. Use Apple’s purchase-history or refund-request process for the Apple account used to make the purchase.

## Troubleshooting

### LiftXP is not saving a workout

Confirm that your device has available storage, then close and reopen LiftXP.

Before deleting or reinstalling the app, remember that LiftXP data is stored locally and may be lost.

### Camera, microphone, or photo access is not working

Open:

**iPhone Settings → LiftXP**

Enable the permission required for the feature.

### The app is freezing or closing

Close and reopen LiftXP. If the problem continues, restart your device and confirm that you are running the latest available version of LiftXP and iOS.

When contacting support, include:

* What you were doing when the problem occurred
* The steps needed to reproduce it
* Your device model
* Your iOS version
* Your LiftXP version
* A screenshot or screen recording, where possible

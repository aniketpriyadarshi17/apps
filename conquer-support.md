## Common Issues

### App is not opening or crashing

Please try updating to the latest version of Conqeur from the App Store. If the issue continues, restart your device and contact support.

### Saved quotes or preferences are missing

Conqeur may store some preferences locally on your device. If the app is deleted or device storage is cleared, some local data may be lost.
